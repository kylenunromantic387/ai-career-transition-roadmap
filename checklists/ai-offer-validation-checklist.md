# AI Offer Validation Checklist

Use this checklist before turning a project into a consulting offer, freelance service, or product wedge.

## Buyer Clarity

- I know who the user or buyer is.
- I can name the workflow pain in one sentence.
- I understand how the problem is handled today.

## Problem Quality

- The problem happens often enough to matter.
- The problem has visible cost in time, money, errors, or decision friction.
- The use case is narrow enough to test quickly.

## AI Fit

- AI is useful here because the problem involves unstructured inputs, ambiguity, or repeated knowledge work.
- A non-AI automation approach would not solve the full problem as well.
- Human review requirements are clear.

## Trust And Delivery

- I understand what would make the buyer distrust the output.
- I can explain the evaluation method simply.
- I know what logs, safeguards, or review steps are needed.

## Proof

- I have a prototype, case study, or walkthrough.
- I can show a before-and-after workflow.
- I can explain trade-offs and limitations honestly.

## Decision Rule

If I cannot clearly explain the buyer, pain, AI fit, trust requirements, and proof asset, I do not yet have a strong offer. I only have an idea.
